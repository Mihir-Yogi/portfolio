Projects

CIMS (cctv information management system)

Our project is a CCTV Information Management System website designed for city depots, featuring distinct admin and user sections. Using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and MySQL, we developed functionalities such as 'Add City' and 'Add City Depot' for streamlined organization. Dedicated forms for NVRs, DVRs, and HDDs, alongside a 'Camera' form with megapixel specifications, enhance data granularity. With AJAX integration, users can submit daily status updates for equipment and generate reports effortlessly. This system enables efficient equipment tracking, fault resolution, and transparent data management for governmental liaison.
